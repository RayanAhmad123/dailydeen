Daily Deen

Daily Islamic Wisdom — short educational videos on Islamic history, language, and teachings.

What it is

Daily Deen is a personal content tool that produces short, respectful educational videos about Islamic history, the Golden Age of science, the Arabic roots of everyday words, and timeless teachings — and publishes them to the creator's own social media accounts (YouTube and TikTok).

How it works

  • Each video is built from a curated, fact-checked topic with an elegant Islamic visual design.
  • The creator authorizes their own TikTok and YouTube accounts via official OAuth.
  • Finished videos are uploaded to the creator's own account through each platform's official API.

Content standards

All videos present verifiable educational facts in a respectful manner. They never depict religious figures and contain no misleading or harmful content.
